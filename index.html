Fonts

Fonts

Headings: Montserrat

Regular, Bold, Italic

Usage: Montserrat is a modern and stylish font that works well for headings and titles on your website. It's clean and easy to read, making it suitable for grabbing users' attention.

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

Paragraphs: Georgia

Regular

Usage: Georgia is a classic serif font that is highly legible in smaller text sizes. It's ideal for body text, paragraphs, and long-form content, providing a professional and traditional appearance.

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

Navigation: Open Sans

Regular, Bold, Italic

Usage: Open Sans is a versatile and friendly sans-serif font. It's excellent for call-to-action buttons, navigation menus, and any text that needs to stand out while maintaining readability.
